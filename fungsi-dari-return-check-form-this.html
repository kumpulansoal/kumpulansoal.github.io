8. (Cash, you)........... This check for me?


Can/Could you cash this check for me?
Answer:

In English, such question above is considered as a request. There are several ways of saying request, which is to ask for someone's help. Here's the example:
- Can you help you?
- Could you help me out?
- Would you assist me?
- Would you mind giving me an assistance?
- Would that be alright / fine if you help me with this issue?

So, the most possible answer for the exercise above is
Can/ could you cash this check, please?

33. change this sentence into negative form​


Jawaban:

2.They do not enjoy a big wave.

3.We don't like to swim at the beach.

4.I do not read a novel this month.

5.You don't tell a bad experience before sleeping.

Penjelasan:

langkah langkah mengubah kalimat negative:

1. (+) Subject + Verb + Object

I spent my holiday in Sanur beach

(-) Subject + Tobe/Auxiliary verb + Not + Verb + Object

I did not spend my holiday in Sanur beach

(?) Tobe/Auxiliary verb + Subject + Verb + Object

Did you spend your holiday in Sanur beach

note : pada kalimat I spent my holiday in Sanur beach, kata spent berubah di kalimat negatif dan kalimat tanya menjadi spend karena jika pada kalimat sudah ada auxiliary/modals (did) maka Verb 2 berubah menjadi Verb 1. Kata I dalam kalimat tanya berubah menjadi "you".

38. 1. what do you call this kind of form2. what institution issued this form3. what is this form for


Untuk menjawab pertanyaan tersebut, terlebih dahulu harus diketahui berbagai macam isian (field) yang ada pada form dan beberapa tulisan lainnya yang bisa dijadikan bahan untuk mengidentifikasi.

George Bank = Menunjukkan bahwa formulir ini dikeluarkan oleh bank yang bernama George Bank.

Bukti Setoran = Judul formulir, menunjukka kegunaan formulir sebagai bukti setoran tunai ke bank. dalam bahasa Inggris disebut sebagai Deposit Slip.

Jenis rekening = type of account, menunjukkan jenis rekening yang mendapatkan setoran.

Nomor rekening = account number

nama pemilik rekening = account holder's name, menunjukkan nama orang yang memiliki rekening yang mendapat setoran tunai

nama penyetor = depositor's name

alamat penyetor = depositor's address

Berdasarkan beberapa isian di kolom sebelah kiri tersebut bisa diambil kesimpulan untuk menjawab pertanyaan berikut:

1. what do you call this kind of form ?

This kind of form is called as "deposit slip" or "bank deposit form"

2. what institution issued this form ?

This form is issued by George Bank

3. what is this form for?

This form is a proof that certain amount of cash deposit has been made and approved by the bank.
