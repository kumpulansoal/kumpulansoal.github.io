Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Turunan fungsi ( f'(x) ) :

[tex]f(x) = a {x}^{n} [/tex]

[tex]f'(x) = an {x}^{n - 1} [/tex]

• f(x) = x³ - 2x² - 100

[tex]f'(x) = 1 \times 3 {x}^{3 - 1} - 2 \times 2 {x}^{2 - 1} [/tex]

[tex]f'(x) = 3 {x}^{2} - 4x[/tex]

[tex]f''(x) = 3 \times 2 {x}^{2 - 1} - 4 \times 1 {x}^{1 - 1} [/tex]

[tex]f''(x) = 6x - 4[/tex]

• g(x) = 4x² + 3x

[tex]g'(x) = 4 \times 2 {x}^{2 - 1} + 3 \times 1 {x}^{1 - 1} [/tex]

[tex]g'(x) = 8x + 3[/tex]

[tex]g''(x) = 8 \times 1 {x}^{1 - 1} [/tex]

[tex]g''(x) = 8[/tex]

• f"(x) = g"(x)

6x - 4 = 8

6x = 8 + 4

6x = 12

x = 12 ÷ 6

x = 2

Jawaban
