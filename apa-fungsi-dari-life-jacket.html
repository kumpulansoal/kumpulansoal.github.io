20. write the procedures of wearing a life jacket on


tuliskan prosedur mengenakan jaket pelampungTrain the use of PFD / Buoy: Choose a brightly colored PFD, make sure no holes or stitches are released on the PFD, as well as existing straps can be installed and removed easily. If your abdomen is larger than the chest, select and use PFD with a larger size. PFD or float is worn like using a vest / jacket. Make sure the strap is properly attached and the head cushion is out. Set the string tightness as comfortable as possible, so the PFD you use is not too narrow or loose. After you finish using PFD, do the following movements: In a standing position, turn your body left and right. Make sure the PFD used does not hamper your gestures and does not experience a shift / change of position. This is indicated by the location of the fixed strap on 1 perpendicular line such as the position of the shirt buttons. If there is a shear, rearrange the strap on each strap. Do not be shy and hesitate to ask your skipper / partner to help arrange this strap tightness. In the sitting position and both legs straightened forward, turn your body left and right and then do a bent motion. Make sure the PFD used does not hamper your gestures. If there is a shift, rearrange each strap.
