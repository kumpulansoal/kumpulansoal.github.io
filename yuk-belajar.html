Jawaban

3 was, wasn't

4 was, was

5 were, weren't

Pembahasan

Tense yang dipakai adalah simple past tense.

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang terjadi di waktu lampau (masa lalu).

Contohnya: last night, last week, yesterday, dll.

Pola simple past tense nominal/non-verbal (predikat berupa to be):

(+) Subject + was/were + complement.

(-) Subject + was/were not + complement.

(?) Was/were + subject + complement?

I, he, she, it (singular) = gunakan wasThey, we, you (plural) = gunakan were
