OBAT GENERIK VS OBAT PATEN

Obat generik merupakan obat yang hak patennya sudah lewat masa berlakunya. Obat generik mengandung zat aktif kualitas yang sama dengan obat paten  sesuai dalam ketentuan Farmakope Indonesia. Obat generik berlogo  mudah sekali untuk diidentifikasi melalui logo lingkaran hijau  bergaris putih dan terdapat tulisan “generik” yang tercantum pada strip maupun dus obat tersebut.  

Sedangkan obat paten merupakan obat keluaran baru yang masih memiliki hak paten  dan diproduksi oleh industri farmasi.  Pada umumnya, masa berlaku obat paten selama 20 tahun.  Setelah lewat masa berlakunya, bisa diproduksi oleh  perusahaan farmasi lainnya.

Stethoscope Usage and Korotkoff Sounds

The stethoscope does not make sounds louder; it simply blocks out extraneous noises so you can better hear the Korotkoff sounds. These sounds are heard through a stethoscope applied over the brachial artery when the blood pressure cuff is deflating.

32. Why aorta is the largest artery


Before that, let us briefly introduce, artery.  An artery carries blood away from the heart.  Additionally, there are two kinds of arteries, pulmonary and systemic.  As its name imply, pulmonary arteries transport blood from the blood to the lungs for oxygenation.  Meanwhile, systemic arteries distribute the blood to the body.

Going back to the question, which artery is the largest and why, the answer would be the aorta.  It is the primary systemic artery that pumps oxygenated blood.  Shortly thereafter, the aorta will transport oxygenated blood away from the heart, then, distribute to every parts of the body.  Since the blood that aorta supplies is high in oxygen content, the blood color is red.

Anatomically, this artery is adjoined to the left ventricle of the heart.  The aorta contracts strongly to push the blood out from the heart.  Further, the aorta has three layers of tissue, the smooth inner layer, thin outer layer and middle layer.  This artery branches out to smaller arteries, ascending aorta and descending aorta.  The ascending aorta supplies blood to the head region and arms.  Likewise, descending aorta distribute blood to lower parts of the body.

The aorta held the honor of being the largest artery because its function and responsibility to the human body are also enormous.  It has to be big in order to supply the whole body system with blood simultaneously.  Moreover, aorta is an elastic organ that made it possible to stretch, thus, aid in maintaining the blood pressure.

On the flipside, this large blood vessel must be healthy all time in order for the continuous blood supply to the body.  In addition, health conditions may arise from the aorta such as aortic stenosis, and abdominal aortic aneurysm.  These conditions are serious and fatal that may lead to death.


33. The heart is a hollow muscle in the middle of the chest that pumps blood around the , body, supplying cells with oxygen and nutrients. A muscular wall, called the septum, divides the heart lengthwise into left and right sides. A valve divides each side into two chambers: an upper atrium and a lower ventricle. When the heart muscle contracts, it squeezes blood through the atria and then through the ventricles. Oxygenated blood from the lungs flows from the pulmonary veins into the left atrium, through. the left ventricle, and then out via the aorta to all parts of the body.
