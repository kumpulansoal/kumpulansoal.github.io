The traffic lights at three differentroad crossings change after every 52seconds, 64 seconds and 102 seconds respectively. If they change simultaneouslyat 7 a.m., at what time will they change simultaneously again?


1. "lupa lupa ingat...ada yg ngomong...kalo bilangan pertama dibagi fpb trus di kali bilangan kedua itu jd kpk" (Cherry1Red, brainly.co.id/tugas/8900829, komen ke-32)

     a = (KPK/120).GCD
        = (2040/120).34
        = 578

2. prime factorizations:
    52 = 2² x 13
    64 = 2⁶
   102 = 2 x 3 x 17
 
    LCM = 102 x 13 x 2⁵
             = 42.432 s
 
    42.432/3600 = 11 hr , 47 mins, 12 sec
 
    7 a.m + 11 hr , 47 mins, 12 sec = 6:47:12 p.m.
