4. Make sentences using should, ought to, and had better. (5 should, 5 ought, and 5 had better).Buatlah kalimat menggunakan should, ought, dan had better. (5 should, 5 ought, dan 5 had better).Tolong jawab ya!​


Jawaban:

Should:

1. You should have eaten that cake before anyone else eat it

2. She should bring her umbrella outside, the weather forecast said it will rain

3. He should have apologized beforehand

4. They should stop annoying random people on the street

5. You should buy that dress, it suits you so well.

Ought to:

1. You ought to listen carefully.

2. We ought to leave now.

3. Lucy ought to go by herself.

4. People ought to be a bit nicer to us.

5. The journey ought to take about 2 hours.

Had better:

1. She'd better wore a black dress, it's a funeral after all

2. We better not wear pink to a formal meeting

3. He better not tell anyone about this incidence

4. You better start working on your assignment now before it's past the deadline

5. They'd better stop yelling in the middle of the night

Penjelasan Singkat:

We use the verbs should, ought to, supposed to and had better to say what you or other people think is the right thing to do. That means these verbs express advice, opinion, criticism or (for had better), warnings or threats.

33. If the team ...a better striker, they would havewon the game. A. Has B. Had C. Had had D. Has had


Jawaban:

B. Had

Penjelasan:

If the team had a better striker, they would havewon the game.

Jawaban:

C

Penjelasan:

Bila kita perhatikan kalimat 'If the team ...a better striker, they would have won the game.' merupakan kalimat dengan pola past future perfect tense.

Dalam kalimat tersebut bentuk past future perfect tense (would) digunakan pada conditional sentence type 3 (kalimat pengandaian) untuk membicarakan sesuatu yang tidak terjadi.

Formulanya adalah;

If + S₁ + Had + V₃ + O, S₂ + would + have + V₃ + O

Sehingga, kalimat yang tepat sesuai formula di atas adalah;

f the team had had a better striker, they would have won the game.

35. write suggestion with had better and shoul​


Jawaban:

Contoh suggestion with Bad better:

-You had better not leave your bag there. Someone might steal it.

-You had better get here soon or you will miss the opening ceremony

Contoh suggestion with Should

– You should call a policeman to help you find your wallet

-You should visit Lake Toba

Penjelasan:

Should biasanya digunakan untuk memberikan saran pada seseorang dan memiliki arti harus atau seharusnya. Should banyak digunakan dalam memberikan masukan atau usulan. Sedangkan had better, memiliki arti yang lebih kuat dibandingkan should. Biasanya berupa bentuk peringatan terhadap suatu hal.

Pembahasan

Shoud dan Had better merupakan modal verbs. Kedua kalimat tersebut digunakan untuk memberikan saran, rekomendasi dan keharusan untuk melakukan sesuatu dan kewajban.
