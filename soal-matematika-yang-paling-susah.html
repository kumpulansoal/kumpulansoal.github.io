11. jawab plis soal matematika susah banget


a + b = 8 . . . . (1)
b + d = 8 . . . (2)
a + c = 13 . . . (3)
c - d = 6 . . . . (4)

a + c = 13
→ a = 13 - c . . . .(5)

a + b = 8
Substitusi persamaan (5)
13 - c + b = 8
b = 8 - 13 + c
b = -5 + c . . . . . (6)

b + d = 8
Substitusi persamaan (6)
-5 + c + d = 8
c + d = 8 + 5
c + d = 13 . . . . . (7)

Eliminasi c pada persamaan (4) dan (7)
c - d = 6
c + d = 13
________ -
-2d = -7
d = 3.5

c + d = 13
c + 3.5 = 13
c = 13 - 3.5
c = 9.5

a + c = 13
a + 9.5 = 13
a = 13 - 9.5
a = 3.5

a + b = 8
3.5 + b = 8
b = 8 - 3.5
b = 4.5

Jadi nilai a, b, c, dan d berturut turut adalah 3.5, 4.5, 9.5, 3.5

sudut ABC = 180° - (30° + 60°)

sudut ABC = 180° - 90°

- Kedua, cari panjang BC (aturan Sinus)

a / Sin A = c / Sin C

BC / Sin BAC = AB / Sin ACB

BC / Sin 30° = 6 / Sin 60°

BC / (1/2) = 6 / (1/2√3)

BC(√3/2) = 3

BC = 3 ÷ (√3/2)

BC = 3 . 2 / √3

BC = (6/√3)

14. Q.Soal matematika paling susah siapa bisa jawab ​


Hasil dari K + A + P adalah 16

PENJELASAN

Misal:

A = apelP = pisangK = kelapa

Diketahui:

A + A + A = 30A + P + P = 18P - K = 2

Ditanyakan:

K + A + P

Jawab:

A + A + A = 30

3A = 30

A = 30 / 3

A = 10

A + P + P = 18

10 + 2P = 18

2P = 18 - 10

2P = 8

P = 8 / 2

P = 4

P - K = 2

4 - K = 2

-K = 2 - 4

-K = -2

K = 2

K + A + P

=> 2 + 10 + 4

=> 12 + 4

=> 16

Jadi, hasil dari K + A + P adalah 16

26. buatkah soal susah matematika dengan hasil 256​


Jawab:

csc 10° sec 20° csc 30° sec 40° csc 50° sec 60° csc 70° sec 80°

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Berdasarkan rumus sudut relasi sin (90° - x) = cos x dan sudut ganda sin 2x = 2 sin x cos x serta sin (180° - x) = sin x

[tex]\displaystyle \csc 10^\circ\sec 20^\circ\csc 30^\circ\sec 40^\circ\csc 50^\circ\sec 60^\circ\csc 70^\circ\sec 80^\circ\\=\frac{1}{\sin 10^\circ\cos 20^\circ\sin 30^\circ\cos 40^\circ\sin 50^\circ\cos 60^\circ\sin 70^\circ\cos 80^\circ}\\=\frac{1}{(\sin 10^\circ\sin 30^\circ\sin 50^\circ\sin 70^\circ)(\cos 20^\circ\cos 40^\circ\cos 60^\circ\cos 80^\circ)}\\=\frac{1}{(\cos 80^\circ\cos 60^\circ\cos 40^\circ\cos 20^\circ)(\cos 20^\circ\cos 40^\circ\cos 60^\circ\cos 80^\circ)}[/tex]

[tex]\displaystyle =\frac{1}{(\cos 20^\circ\cos 40^\circ\cos 60^\circ\cos 80^\circ)^2}\\=\frac{1}{\left ( \frac{1}{2}~\frac{\sin 40^\circ}{2\sin 20^\circ}~\frac{\sin 80^\circ}{2\sin 40^\circ}~\frac{\sin 160^\circ}{2\sin 80^\circ} \right )^2}\\=\frac{1}{\left ( \frac{\sin 20^\circ}{16\sin 20^\circ} \right )^2}\\=\frac{1}{\frac{1}{256}}\\=256[/tex]
