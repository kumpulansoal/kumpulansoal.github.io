12. Apa fungsi prinsip half tone dalam karya seni


Jawaban:

Prinsip half tone dalam karya seni digunakan untuk menciptakan efek gradasi atau peralihan antara dua atau lebih nilai kegelapan atau kecerahan dalam gambar. Fungsi utama dari prinsip half tone adalah untuk menyampaikan volume, tekstur, dan dimensi pada suatu objek yang dihasilkan melalui pengaturan titik-titik kecil dengan jumlah yang berbeda-beda.

Prinsip half tone sering digunakan dalam teknik seni seperti cetak, fotografi, grafis, dan seni digital. Dalam cetakan, prinsip half tone digunakan untuk mengubah gambar dengan kecerahan dan kegelapan yang berbeda menjadi titik-titik kecil yang teratur. Dalam fotografi, prinsip half tone digunakan untuk menghasilkan efek ilusi keberagaman gradasi dan nuansa dalam gambar. Dalam seni digital, prinsip half tone dapat digunakan untuk memberikan dimensi dan kedalaman pada gambar dengan mengatur pola titik sebagai elemen pengolahannya.

20. cara membuat minuman lemon squash memakai bahasa inggris


How to make lemon squash

1. Fill a bottle with sugar and pour lemon juice to come up till the top.

2. Sun it for a few days, shaking the bottle 3-4 times a day, till totally dissolved. When dissolved, you use it.

3. Alternatively put 4 cups sugar and 3 cups water in a pan and dissolved sugar, by stirring over low heat.

4. When dissolved, bring to a boil and cook for about 5 minutes over high heat or till one-thread consistency is reached.

5. Take it off the heat and mix in 1-1/1/2 cups of lemon juice (according to taste), store in sterilized air tight bottles.

37. what is the movement used in squash sport​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

In squash, several types of movements are commonly used by players to navigate the court and reach the ball effectively. These movements include:

1. **Running:** Players need to run quickly and efficiently to reach the ball before it bounces twice. Short sprints and rapid changes in direction are common in squash.

2. **Sideways Shuffling:** This movement involves shuffling the feet sideways along the court, allowing the player to maintain a balanced and ready position to react to the ball.

3. **Lunging:** Lunging involves a rapid forward step, usually accompanied by a split step or jump, to reach the ball close to the front wall. This movement requires balance and quick reaction time.

4. **Backpedaling:** Players often need to backpedal when the ball is hit deep into the back corners of the court. Backpedaling allows players to maintain their position and prepare for the next shot.

5. **Crossing Over:** In situations where players need to switch directions rapidly, they may use the crossover step, where one foot crosses in front of the other to change direction efficiently.

6. **Volleying:** In addition to moving to reach the ball, players also need to volley the ball, hitting it in the air before it bounces. Volleying requires precise timing and hand-eye coordination.

7. **Boast and Drive:** These are specific shots in squash that involve hitting the ball off the front wall at different angles. Players may need to move to different positions on the court to execute these shots effectively.

8. **Tin and Tinning:** These movements refer to hitting the ball into the tin (bottom part of the front wall) or aiming for the tin when playing a drop shot. Players may adjust their movements and shot selection to execute these tactics.

9. **Ghosting:** Ghosting drills are practice routines where players simulate different movements and shots on the court to improve their agility, footwork, and conditioning.

Effective movement in squash is crucial for maintaining control of the game, reaching the ball in time, and conserving energy. Players often practice these movements intensively to improve their on-court performance.

Set dan angka permainan Sunting

Sebuah lapangan skuas.

Setiap permainan terdiri dari lima set, tetapi jika salah seorang pemain telah memenangkan tiga set, maka ia adalah pemenangnya.

Ada dua macam penilaian angka: sistem tradisional menyatakan bahwa hanya pemain yang melakukan servis (orang pertama yang mulai memukul bola) yang berhak memperoleh angka jika pemain lawan gagal mengembalikan bola hasil pukulan sang pemain atau melakukan kesalahan, sementara sistem lainnya yang disebut sistem Amerika, menyatakan bahwa pemenang setiap rally berhak mendapatkan angka tanpa memedulikan apakah sang pemenang adalah orang yang melakukan servis atau bukan. Dalam pertandingan internasional, sistem penilaian angka yang digunakan adalah sistem tradisional. Menggunakan sistem tradisional, setiap set akan dimenangi jika seorang pemain mencapai 9 poin, sementara dalam sistem Amerika, poin yang harus dicapai adalah 11 poin.
