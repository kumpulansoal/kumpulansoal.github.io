25. QUESTION ( WH QUESTION )chane become indirect speech !(1.) Aurelia said to Fino ," Where can i buy this that now ?"(2.) Riana asked ubbay ," when will we join the students organization? "(3.) Wida said to carel, " why you mary me?"(4.) Yumma said to faizur ," when shal i come to you house? "(5.) Eko said to yuni ,' what could you do for you future?"(6.) Jhon asked mary ," why do you get angry with me ?"(7.) He asked me ," How will you go there ?"​


INDIRECT SPEECH

(1.) Aurelia said to Fino ," Where can i buy this hat now ?"

↪ Aurelia asked Fino where she could buy that hat at that time.

(2.) Riana asked ubbay ," when will we join the students organization? "

↪ Riana asked Ubbay when they would join the students organization.

(3.) Wida said to carel, " why you mary me?"

↪ Wida asked Carel why he married her.

(4.) Yumma said to faizur ," when shall i come to your house? "

↪ Yumma asked Faizur when she should come to his house.

(5.) Eko said to yuni ,' what could you do for you future?"

↪Eko asked Yuni what she could have done for her future.

(6.) Jhon asked mary ," why do you get angry with me ?"

↪ Jhon asked Mary why she got angry with him.

(7.) He asked me ," How will you go there ?"

↪ He asked me how I would go there.

EXPLANATION

Reported speech[Indirect speech] adalah ungkapan pelaporan ulang dari apa yang telah disampaikan oleh si pembicara utama kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi tersirat.

Hal² yang diperhatikan dalam merubah direct speech ke reported speech :

~ Cermati jenis kalimatnya, apakah termasuk afirmasi, tanya dan imperatif [saran, nasihat, larangan, perintah].

~ Sejajarkan dengan sudut pandang [siapa yg diajak bicara, siapa yg membicarakannya]

~ Perhatikan jenis reporting verb yg diminta; pakai bentuk PAST [told, asked, warned] atau PRESENT [tell, ask, warn].

Jika menggunakan "Present simple, Present perfect, Future", noun clause tdk mengalami perubahan tenses.

Sebaliknya, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked, warned}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift

baik dari keterangan waktu, petunjuk maupun tenses.

Simple present ➛Simple past

Simple past ➛Past perfect

Present perfect ➛Past perfect

Past continuous ➛Past perfect continuous

Now ➛at that time

Today ➛ that day

Yesterday ➛ the day before

Tomorrow ➛ the next day

Last week ➛ the previous week

Exp.

D.S : Patih Gajah Mada asked his soldiers, " what could you do for your future?"

➡ Patih Gajah Mada asked his soldiers what they could have done for their future.

Elaboration;

Kalimat tanya pada direct speech dirubah ke bentul pernyataan.Reporting verb yg digunakan adalah bentuk past "asked", jadi ada perubahan tenses pada noun clause verb-nya, di mana yg awalnya menggunakan simple past "Could do" menjadi past perfect "Could have done."Pronomina juga berubah berdasarkan sudut pandang, meliputi siapa yg diajak bicara dan siapa yg membicarakannya. Awalnya menggunakan kata ganti orang kedua "you" berubah menjadi kata ganti orang ketiga jamak "they."
