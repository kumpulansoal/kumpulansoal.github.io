8. Abel membeli 3 iced green tea dan 4 iced milk tea seharga Rp120.000,-, sementara Brigitta membeli 3 iced milk tea dan 2 iced coffee seharga Rp105.000,-. Jika kemudian diketahui bahwa Cindy membeli 3 iced green tea dan 1 iced coffee seharga Rp90.000,-, berapa yang harus dibayar jika Daniel membeli 4 iced green tea, 4 iced milk tea dan 3 iced coffee? a. Rp. 200.000 b. Rp. 210.000 c. Rp. 220.000 d. Rp. 230.000


Jawaban:

D. 230.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Misal :

Green tea = x

Milk tea = y

Coffe = z

3x + 4y = Rp120.000 (pers 1)

3y + 2z = Rp105.000 (pers 2)

3x + z = Rp90.000 (pers 3)

Eliminasi Z pada pers 2 dan 3

3y + 2z = 105.000 (dikali 1) = 3y + 2z = 105.000

3x + z = 90.000 (dikali 2) = 6x + 2z = 180.000

3y + 2z = 105.000

6x + 2z = 180.000

(Dikurangi) = 3y - 6x = -75.000 (pers 4)

Eliminasi x pada pers 1 dan 4

3x + 4y = 120.000 (dikali 2) = 6x + 8y = 240.000

3y - 6x = -75.000 (dikali 1) = 3y - 6x = -75.000

6x + 8y = 240.000

3y - 6x = -75.000

(Ditambah) = 11y = 165.000

y = 15.000

Substitusikan y = 15.000 ke pers 1

3x + 4(15.000) = Rp120.000

3x = 120.000 - 60.000

3x = 60.000

x = 20.000

Substitusikan x = 20.000 ke pers 3

3(20.000) + z = Rp90.000

z = 90.000 - 60.000

z = 30.000

Harga green tea = 20.000

Harga milk tea = 15.000

Harga coffe = 30.000

Daniel membeli 4 iced green tea, 4 iced milk tea dan 3 ice coffe = 4(20.000) + 4(15.000) + 3(30.000) = 80.000 + 60.000 + 90.000 = 230.000

40. Cara membuat kue cubit green tea dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Ingredients
3 eggs
150 grams of sugar
50 grams of margarine, melt
200 ml of plain liquid milk
300 gr multipurpose flour
1/2 tsp double acting baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
vanilla
greentea powder
Step
Mixer of sugar and eggs to expand, enter vanilla and greentea powder

  
Lower the speed of the mixer, add flour, salt and bakibg powder that has been sifted

  
Add liquid margarine and liquid milk, mix well. NOTE: I skip liquid milk, I replace it with chocolatos greentea brewed with 200ml of water. The green color does not appear, but there is a taste of greenery. It is better to use greentea powder in TBK

  
Leave the mixture for 30 minutes, cover with a wet cloth.

  
Heat the cubit cake mold with low heat tends to be medium. Rub with thin margarine. Pour the mixture until it is half cooked, topping it. Cook until cooked

  
Can also be lifted when it's still half cooked, cubit cake is more moist

arti:
Bahan-bahan
3 Butir Telur
150 gr gula pasir
50 gr margarin, cairkan
200 ml susu cair plain
300 gr terigu serbaguna
1/2 sdt baking powder double acting
1/2 sdt garam
vanili
serbuk greentea
Langkah
Mixer gula dan telur hingga mengembang, masukkan vanili dan serbuk greentea


Turunkan kecepatan mixer, Masukkan terigu, garam dan bakibg powder yang sudah diayak


Masukkan margarin cair dan susu cair, aduk rata. NOTE: saya skip susu cair, saya ganti dengan chocolatos greentea yang diseduh dengan air 200ml. Warna hijau tidak muncul, tapi ada rasa greenteanya. Sebaiknya memang menggunakan bubuk greentea yang ada di TBK


Diamkan adonan selama 30 menit, tutup dengan kain basah.


Panaskan cetakan kue cubit dengan api kecil cenderung sedang. Oles dengan margarin tipis-tipis. Tuang adonan tunggu hingga setengah matang, beri topping. Masak hingga matang


Bisa juga diangkat pas masih setengah matang, kue cubit lebih moist
